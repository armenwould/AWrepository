Why is this program being developed?

The Generator

My project is a white noise generator. The program will analyze noise and produce a white noise to cancel the sound out.
The main features of the project will be:

  • audio input hardware (microphone)
  • Sound analyzer
  • White noise output

The Sound Analyzer will detect the peaks of a sound input and use that data to excentuate the white noise to cancel the sound out.
